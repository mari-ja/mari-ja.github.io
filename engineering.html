Marija Katic, PhD


Management and Engineering


Marija works as a Project Manager and Lead Software Developer at Birkbeck, University of London.

In addition, she has experience of open-source development as a Lead Developer. At Birkbeck's Centre for Technology and Publishing, in collaboration with Professor Martin Eve, she developed a tool that facilitates collaborative translation of web pages.

She worked on a few software development projects in Siemens in Croatia, and a few business-oriented projects at the Faculty of Electrical Engineering and Computing, University of Zagreb.
